How it works
Step 1
Route → duration
Your origin and destination map to a pre-computed travel time (minutes) based on real Colombo road distances and typical traffic speeds (~22 kmph).
Step 2
ML base fare prediction
A Linear Regression model (R²=0.87) uses the ride duration, vehicle type, and contextual features to predict the base fare.
Step 3
Dynamic pricing formula
Final fare = base × demand_mult × supply_mult. Three-zone quantile formula with 0.8–1.5× caps, matching your notebook's corrected implementation.
